Renting a Dress: Instructions

  • Step1

    Select the dress you want

    from our dress collection, find the model of the dress you would like to rent. More dresses are available on our Facebook and Instagram page.

  • Step2

    Get in contact with us via Whatsapp

    You can call or Whatsapp us.


  • Step3

    Book a Day to rent the dress.

    During the wedding season, we receive a lot of orders so timing is everything. Give us a date when you want to use the dress you want so we can keep it in our records.

  • Step4

    Pay for the dress

    Your booking is cemented only when you pay for your the service we will provide. If another bride pays before you for the same dress for the same date, we will prioritize the paid order!

  • Step5

    Collect the dress.

    If you have booked and paid for your dress, collect your dress 2 days before the day of your wedding. Make sure to bring your receipt as proof of payment! We check for damages before you pick the dress for your event.

  • Step6

    Return the dress

    Please return the dress to us within 2 days after your wedding. We will check for damages together with you when you return the dress. If damages are found, they have to be paid for! Damages we check for include permanent stains and torns.
